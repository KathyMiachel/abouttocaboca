What is Toca Boca?

Toca Boca is a Swedish game developer specializing in digital toys for children. Instead of competitive challenges, the company’s games focus on roleplay, exploration, and self-expression. Each title is designed to be child-friendly, ad-free, and easy to navigate without written instructions.

Over the years, the company has released dozens of apps in categories such as cooking (Toca Kitchen), styling (Toca Hair Salon), health (Toca Doctor), and everyday life (Toca Life: World). The games often reflect real-world activities, allowing players to experiment without real-life consequences.
